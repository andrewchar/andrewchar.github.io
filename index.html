about me

Hey, I'm Andrew, a carpenter turned web developer. My passion for building and my attention to detail gives me the ability to pay close attention to every pixel to ensure that in any interface I develop is stunning, yet functional. I am a front-end developer based in Pembroke, Ontario. I'm self-taught and always growing my skills, learning something new every day.

I specialize in Front-End Development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript). I have also done some work with PHP and WordPress.
