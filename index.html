about me

Hey I'm Andrew, carpenter turned web developer. My passion for building and my attention to detail gives me the ability to pay close attention to every pixel to ensure that in any interface I develop is stunning, yet functional. I am a front-end developer based in Pembroke, Ontario. I'm self-taught and always growing my skills, learning something new every day.
